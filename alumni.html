Jason C. Tan

BS'11, ECE
  • Founder
  • 1st President
  • 1st Generation

Jason founded RICE Crew in the fall of 2010 and served as the 1st President. Frustrated with the quality of hip hop dances at the UR, Jason set out to create a new dance team that would bring a new level of dance to the Rochester community. Under his leadership, having lots of fun while putting together a great performance became a core value of the group. Since leaving the UR, he has started studying computer science to purse entrepreneurial endeavors.
